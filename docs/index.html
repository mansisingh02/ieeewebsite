About Us

"The art of communication is the language of leadership."
?We believe in making an individual a master of words, a master of their communication skills.
Our objective is to raise the morale of students and
work towards creating a society of confident and ambitious people.
Starting out as a small initiative, today we are proud to be
one of the best chapters in VIT, with hopes of growing and
achieving even greater heights.

Our working principle

• IEEE PCS helps its members to develop their skills both technically and as great word masters.
• IEEE PCS will provide resources in all possible Resources and motivate them to excel in technical and orating fields.
• They will be taking up events which will be both mind boggling and highly innovative.
•IEEE PCS will also help and guide them towards technical communication and how that will benefit engineers in the long run.

Our background

IEEE-Professional Communication Society is a place where communication
meets technology. IEEE-PCS is a welcoming family
and this reflects when we participate in literary fests
and often emerge as victors due to our commendable team spirit. Our 4 departments,
or must we say, our 4 pillars are: Management, Editorial, Design, and Technical teams
who work towards achieving one common goal: to become 'Masters of Communication'.

Our vision

We as members of the chapter resonate with the quote:
"The art of communication is the language of leadership" and so, we hone our
communication skills to become better orators and in turn, better leaders.

Technical Housie

Technical Housie is one of the most exuberant events held in VIT and is mirrored as the usual Housie game but through an interesting technological vortex. The participants are required to choose the correct options from the set of answers provided and simultaneously fold the respective number of the option chosen on the Housie card. The ones who meet the respective requisites on their cards are declared as the winners in those respective requisites. Time, knowledge and a pinch of luck are all that you need.
